## What this is about

I work with customers on Microsoft 365 Copilot and AI adoption every day. What I keep noticing: most organizations don't struggle with the *technology* — they struggle with **getting people to actually use it**.

So I started building things on the side: ready-to-deploy Copilot agents, complete adoption campaigns, foundational learning paths, and small tools that take the friction out of agent development. Everything here is **community work** — no support, no warranty, MIT license — but built based on real struggles.

The goal is simple: **lower the barrier to entry**, especially for the folks who aren't deep into adoption yet. Take a template, customize it, ship it, scale your AI usage.

## 🧪 Currently exploring: Copilot Cowork (Frontier)

Microsoft's newest Copilot capability, **Copilot Cowork**, is moving fast — and hands-on practitioner content is still scarce. **[CoworkCustoms](https://github.com/JaysonBucket/CoworkCustoms)** is my open lab: practitioner notes, decision guides, and architectural comparisons from real experimentation.

What you'll find:

- **NEW** [Custom skill DailyOps](https://github.com/JaysonBucket/Cowork-DailyOps) - Personal daily-operations system as a Cowork Custom Skill — tasks, customers, stakeholders, and a mail-capture loop for customer-facing roles.
- **[Chat vs. Skill vs. Agent — full comparison](https://github.com/JaysonBucket/CoworkCustoms/blob/main/docs/Chat-Skill-Agent-Comparison.md)** — feature-by-feature breakdown and decision tree across Copilot Chat, Cowork Custom Skills, Agent Builder Agents, Declarative Agents, and Custom Agents in Copilot Studio
- **[Custom Skills basics](https://github.com/JaysonBucket/CoworkCustoms/blob/main/docs/CustomSkills.md)** — folder structure, schema, and why Anthropic-style skills can't be ported 1:1
- **[Adding custom skills, the right way](https://github.com/JaysonBucket/CoworkCustoms/blob/main/docs/AddCustomSkills.md)** — two import paths, plus how to run quality checks via the `/skills` command
- **[Curated sources](https://github.com/JaysonBucket/CoworkCustoms/blob/main/docs/sources.md)** — Microsoft's official docs, FAQ, roadmap, and Frontier program info

> ⚠️ Frontier features evolve fast. These notes reflect hands-on observations and may change with the product. Treat as practitioner perspective, not official Microsoft guidance.
