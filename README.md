## What this is about

I work with customers on Microsoft 365 Copilot and AI adoption every day. What I keep noticing: most organizations don't struggle with the *technology* — they struggle with **getting people to actually use it**.

So I started building things on the side: ready-to-deploy Copilot agents, complete adoption campaigns, foundational learning paths, and small tools that take the friction out of agent development. Everything here is **community work** — no support, no warranty, MIT license — but built from real struggles.

The goal is simple: **lower the barrier to entry**, especially for the folks who aren't deep into adoption yet. Take a template, customize it, ship it, scale your AI usage.
